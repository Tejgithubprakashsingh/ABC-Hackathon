Snapshots of the prototype

Proposed Snapshots:
Dashboard Overview:

A snapshot of the main dashboard displaying key metrics like AI model performance, recent blockchain transactions, smart contract statuses, and system health.
Data Submission Interface:

A snapshot of the interface where users can submit data for AI processing. This includes fields for data entry, a preview of the data, and a submission button.
AI Analysis Results:

A snapshot showing the results generated by the AI model. This includes charts, graphs, and detailed analysis, with options to download or explore the data.
Smart Contract Execution Monitor:

A snapshot showing the status of various smart contracts, including whether they are pending, executed, or failed. It might include a timeline or logs of contract executions.
Blockchain Data Explorer:

A snapshot of the interface allowing users to browse data stored on the blockchain, with filters and search options for specific transactions or records.
